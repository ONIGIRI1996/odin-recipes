Brownies

Description

A really chewy and good brownie, served with a cup of coffee or a glass of milk, surely no one can resist? Delicious!

Ingredients

  • 6 dl sugar
  • 8 tbsp Cocoa Powder
  • 3 dl wheat flour
  • 2 tsp vanilla sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 300 g butter
  • 4 pcs. egg

Steps

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients in a baking bowl
  2. Melt the butter in a small saucepan and add the butter to the mixture.
  3. Add eggs and mix everything well.
  4. Grease a small frying pan, approx. 25x35 cm. Then line the pan with baking paper. Pour the batter into the long pan and spread it out.
  5. Bake the cake in the middle of the oven at 150-160 Â°C for approx. 30 minutes.
  6. Cool the cake completely and cut it into pieces. Serve the cake with fresh raspberries, a raspberry coulis or a small sprinkling of icing sugar. An iced coffee or a glass of cold milk also tastes good.
